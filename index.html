★ About Us ★

For over three decades, Custom Paint Works has been the trusted shop for collectors, hot-rodders, and weekend warriors who want their classic brought back the right way — panel by panel, coat by coat.

Every vehicle that rolls into our shop gets hand-stripped, hand-blocked, and finished with show-quality paint. No shortcuts. No production-line work. Just honest craftsmanship on steel that deserves it.

Whether it's a frame-off restoration or a fresh candy-apple respray, we treat your car like it's our own.

  • ★ 30+ years of restoration experience
  • ★ Show-quality finishes
  • ★ Full in-house bodywork
  • ★ Insured & guaranteed
